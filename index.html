Welcome to Living Life On The Veg

At Living Life On The Veg, we are passionate about promoting a plant-based lifestyle and raising awareness about the benefits of a plant based diet. Our mission is to inspire and support individuals in adopting a plant based lifestyle!

Why Choose Veganism?

Plant based is more than just a diet; it is a conscious choice to abstain from consuming animal products. By following a plant based lifestyle, you contribute to the reduction of animal suffering and promote health. Moreover, plants and herbs has numerous health benefits, as a well-planned plant based diet can provide all the essential nutrients needed for a healthy lifestyle, including protein, iron, calcium, and vitamins.

The Impact Of Plant Based Foods

By embracing raw herbs and plants, you become part of a global movement that aims to address pressing environmental issues. Animal agriculture is a leading cause of greenhouse gas emissions, deforestation, and water pollution. Choosing plant-based alternatives helps reduce our carbon footprint and preserve natural resources.
