About Us

Living Life On The Veg is dedicated to spreading awareness and knowledge about the benefits of a plant-based lifestyle. We believe in the power of veganism to transform lives and create a better world for all.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide valuable resources, support, and inspiration to individuals interested in embracing veganism. We strive to make the transition to a plant-based lifestyle as seamless and enjoyable as possible, empowering people to make conscious choices that align with their values.

Our Vision

We envision a future where veganism is accessible and embraced by people from all walks of life. We aim to create a community that celebrates compassion, sustainability, health and well-being, while advocating for the rights and welfare of animals.

Our Inspirations

Our journey has been inspired by remarkable individuals who have made significant contributions to the Vegan/Alkaline Diet:

  • Dr Sebi: has inspired us with his advocacy for alkaline-based diets. His profound knowledge and ability to cure over 70 people of various diseases through plant-based healing showcase the transformative power of a alkaline based lifestyle in improving health and well-being.
  • Yahki Awakened: As a biochemist and herbalist, Yahki Awakened has played a vital role in educating people about the benefits of plant-based nutrition and natural remedies. His commitment to sharing knowledge and empowering individuals aligns with our mission to provide valuable resources and support on the journey to veganism and herbalism.
  • Fit Shortie: Fit Shortie's inspiring journey as a raw vegan for 8 years has demonstrated the incredible vitality and energy that can be achieved through a plant-based diet. There dedication to nourishing the body with whole, unprocessed foods resonates with our belief in the power of conscious choices for optimal well-being.
  • Eat To Live, Not To Die: Eat To Live, Not To Die has been at the forefront of promoting an alkaline-based diet since 2013. Their emphasis on the healing properties of alkaline foods and the importance of pH balance in achieving vibrant health aligns with our goal to educate and empower individuals to make informed choices for their well-being.
